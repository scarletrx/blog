UMAYAMU is a collection of exquisite discoveries curated by luxury lifestyle writer, Aamaal Abdul-Malik.

Fueled by an affinity for beautiful things, UMAYAMU guides you in discovering beauty from all over the world, from places like Africa, the Mediterranean, the Middle East, and South East Asia. Twice a week, features of fashiono, jewellery, accessories, decor, and more are presented, revealing the dedication, talent, and beauty some of the world's most beautiful things.

An advocate of “conscientious luxury”, Aamaal stays abreast of the newest developments in sustainable luxury fashion, jewellery, accessories, interiors, and travel and destinations. She firmly believes sustainability and luxury can co-exist, resulting in beautifully rich experiences.

In addition to UMAYAMU, Aamaal is a contributing editor at Eat Love Savor Magazine, covering rare and stunning jewellery, timepieces, and luxury hospitality and travel destinations from the Middle East, Africa, and South Asia. At Sweet Modesty, she writes about breathtakingly elegant luxury fashion from African and Middle Eastern brands and designers.
