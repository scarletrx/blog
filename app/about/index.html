UMAYAMU is a collection of exquisite discoveries curated by luxury strategist, Aamaal Abdul-Malik.

Fueled by an affinity for beautiful things, UMAYAMU guides you with discovering beauty from Africa, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia. Twice a week, features on fashion, jewellery, and events are presented, revealing the people, cultures, and techniques behind some of the world's most beautiful things.

As an advocate of integrating natural materials, sustainable practices, and social change within the context of luxury, Aamaal stays abreast of the newest developments in ethical fashion, jewellery, accessories, beauty & fragrance, food & beverage, and travel. She firmly believes sustainability and luxury can co-exist, resulting in beautifully rich experiences.

In addition to UMAYAMU, Aamaal is the Contributing Fine Jewelry Editor at Eat Love Savor Magazine, covering rare and stunning jewellery across the ages.
