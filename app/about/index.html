UMAYAMU is a collection of exquisite discoveries curated by luxury lifestyle editor, Aamaal Abdul-Malik.

Fueled by an affinity for beautiful things, UMAYAMU guides you in discovering beauty from Africa, the Mediterranean, the Middle East, and South East Asia. Twice a week, features on fashion, jewellery, accessories, decor, and more are presented, revealing the people, cultures, and techniques behind some of the world's most beautiful items and destinations.

As an advocate of integrating natural materials, sustainable practices, and social change within the context of luxury, Aamaal stays abreast of the newest developments in ethical fashion, jewellery, accessories, beauty & fragrance, food & beverage, and travel. She firmly believes sustainability and luxury can co-exist, resulting in beautifully rich experiences.

In addition to UMAYAMU, Aamaal is a contributing editor at Eat Love Savor Magazine, covering rare and stunning jewellery and luxury lifestyle brands and services from the Middle East, Africa, and South Asia. At Sweet Modesty, she writes about breathtakingly elegant luxury fashion from African and Middle Eastern brands and designers.
